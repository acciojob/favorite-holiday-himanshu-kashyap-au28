Manali


Manali is a popular hill station in Himachal Pradesh known for its scenic beauty, mountains, and pleasant climate.

Top Attractions

  1. Solang Valley
  2. Rohtang Pass
  3. Hadimba Temple
  4. Old Manali
  5. Beas River

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu
  • Thukpa
  • Babru
  • Trout Fish
  • Mittha

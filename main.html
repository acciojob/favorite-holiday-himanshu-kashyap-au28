Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station in Himachal Pradesh, India, known for its snow-capped mountains, scenic valleys, adventure sports, and peaceful environment. It is a popular destination for nature lovers and tourists seeking relaxation and adventure.

  1. Solang Valley – Famous for adventure activities and snow views.
  2. Rohtang Pass – A high mountain pass offering breathtaking scenery.
  3. Hadimba Temple – An ancient temple surrounded by cedar forests.
  4. Old Manali – Known for its culture, cafés, and calm atmosphere.
  5. Beas River – A scenic river ideal for photography and relaxation.
  • Siddu – A traditional steamed bread from Himachal Pradesh.
  • Thukpa – A warm noodle soup popular in cold weather.
  • Babru – A local stuffed bread served with chutney.
  • Trout Fish – Freshly cooked river fish, a regional specialty.
  • Mittha – A sweet dish made with rice and dry fruits.
